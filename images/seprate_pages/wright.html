Wright Research, founded in 2019, is a revolutionary data and AI-driven quantitative model that is transforming the investment landscape. Established by seasoned quant researchers, the company utilises data science and machine learning algorithms to develop scalable, quant-driven investment solutions specifically designed for curated for various segments of investors. By offering a digital investment platform that curates portfolios and employs predictive models, Wright Research empowers individuals to grow their wealth efficiently and accessibly. The company's core mission is to democratize access to AI-driven investment products, breaking down the barriers that have traditionally made wealth creation the privilege of a select few.

Through its innovative research and customer-centric approach, Wright Research has earned the trust of a diverse clientele, ranging from tech-savvy professionals and first-time investors to high-net-worth individuals and institutional investors. This trust was further validated when the company successfully raised USD 1 million in its seed round in March 2023.

Under the leadership of Sonam Srivastava, the founder, Wright Research has developed a distinguished investment platform that caters to a wide range of risk profiles and investment objectives. The platform’s offerings span from AI to factor and momentum investing, making sophisticated investment strategies easily understandable and accessible to all investors. Wright Research didn’t just get recognition by Forbes India and D GLOBALIST in DGEM 2023 as a select 200 company, but also emerged as a leading disruptor in the investment and portfolio management industry, evidenced by the rapid scaling of its Portfolio Management Service (PMS) to USD 20 million.

Wright Research's impressive growth and accomplishments have led to its selection as part of the 8th Cohort of the Google for Startups Accelerator: India, securing cloud credits valued at USD 350K. The company has also formed strategic partnerships with Google as part of the AI accelerator and with renowned financial institutions such as Nuvama Wealth, Emkay Wealth, Philip Capital, and Orbis Financials. Additionally, Wright Research has collaborated with PMS distribution partners, including SBNRI, PMS Bazaar, and PMS AIF World, among others. With assets under management, now reaching USD 100 million, the company plans to penetrate major foreign financial markets by developing competitive products tailored to international demand.

As global financial markets continue to evolve and become more interconnected, Wright Research remains committed to modernizing the investment process and making wealth creation accessible to all.
